Home page

Welcome to UFC 309! The long await return of John 'Bones' Jones. In this card you will see many amazing fights. Starting with the Main Event Of the Evening. John Jones vs Stipe Miocic this is a fight the fans have been waiting for over a year now.

Back in November 2023 Jones and Miocic were scheduled for a fight. When Suddenly John was out, he had torn his pectoral muscles straight of the bone needing surgery to repair. But after a yearlong break, we finally get the match up of the greatest of all time (Jones) VS the greatest Heavy weight of all time (Miocic). As many know Jones is widely regarded as the greatest mixed martial artist to ever live. He made the jump up from light heavy weight (205 lb.) to heavy weight (206-265lb.) immediately capturing the belt back in 2023.

Jones looks to defend his title against the greatest Heavy Weight of All Time Stipe Miocic. By beating Miocic thus would cement Jones his legacy as the greatest fighter of all time. The man looking to forte that plan and regain his belt however is Stipe Miocic. Who, as we all know is regarded as the greatest of all time.

Main Event

Now time for a breakdown for the Main Event of the Evening. As stated before Jones is looking to cement his legacy as the greatest of all time. His ability to read and deceive fighters is unlike any other fighter we have ever seen. Competing in the UFC since Jones was only 19 Jones quickly rows to fame becoming the youngest UFC Champion ever at just 21 years old. He has the most title fights in UFC history (16).Although he has a very troubled past he still remains the fan favorite and favorite for the fight.

The man looking to take John down and show the world he still got it is Stipe Miocic. Know for his beautiful striking, amazing resilin's, and impressive grappling. He took a 2 year break off from the sport after losing to Francis Ngannu by scary knockout. Stipe is ready to step back into the ring in order to prove he still is the king of the heavy weight division.

Will Jones be able to complete is conquest and show everyone why he is considered the G.O.A.T ? or will Stipe come back for his belt with a vengeance. Showing age is only a number ? Tune in November 16th, 2024 to find out.
